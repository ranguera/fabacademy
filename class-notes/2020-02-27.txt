- bigger than 45 deg angles require support - more material, more time
- think about layers. tension through layers is weak, tension across layers is good
- depending on the material, remote extruder or direct extruder is preferred- 
- make sure the model is water tight, a closed mesh
- no crossing meshes
- if we have different objects, put them on different files and let the slicer arrange
- minimum size 0.5mm or the slicer wont take it
- rhino will tell you if a mesh is open or not
- meshmixer - meshlab to close open meshes
- in layers you can go as low as 0.1mm, so you can build more details on height than on the x,z plane
- zbrush can slice and close meshes
