- shear, not pulling up

LASER CUTTER
- check types of joints on class doc
- chamfer: good for cardboard
- pinned: strongest holding force
- finger: easy, holding force friction
- wedge: stronges right angle, com un baldo
- hole must be less than =- .005" inches
- recommended: makes a series of slots each slightly bigger to find the right cutout
- most common: CO2
- air flow through the nozzle, exhaust through the bed
- laser cutters want to ignite.
- si comenca a cremar, obre la tapa i el laser es para. pero si hi ha molt foc lalimentes amb oxigen. llavors posar manta
- si el cartro es doblega es que es heavy duty. si es parteix en angle recte no
- cardboard to prototype
- despres de tallar, deixar la tapa tancara una mica pq sacabi de ventilar
- acrylic - pots fondre materials i escalfarlo per doblegarlo
- when making joints with acrylic, chamfer them to make them more resistant. angles rectes es volen trencar
- PVC is nasty - releases chlorine and etches the laser. if you burn the material and you see color, don;t put it in the laser
- high rate: smooth cutting, but materials that melt or burn bad.
